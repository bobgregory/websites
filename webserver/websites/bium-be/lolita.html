Japans "kawaii culture" came about after 1899 with formation of many female only secondary schools. Providing education for these girls created female-only spaces where the expectation of marriage and children was postponed, within this space the shoujo youth culture began.
ベルサイユのばら The Rose of Versailles! Such an incredibly influencial manga. It came out in 1972 and shaped
both the manga world and japanese culture, introducing Japans mainstream to French Roccoco aestectics, this manga was a core influence on both Japanese tourists and lolita fashion.
Lolitas first printed mention was in fashion magazine Ryukou Tsushin's september 1987 issue, this 2 page spread isnt the most complimentary...
